Final Project

Moran_DrawingWords_FinalProject

This is my final project for CSC 106 this semester. For my final project I made an educational app
that allows the user to choose their favorite animal and learn how to spell them. Even though this is a fairly
simple program, I believe it ould be helpful for younger children. To use all you have to do is press the button
for the animal, and choose the color that you want to spell it in.
